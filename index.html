《在亿万世界中，我只遇见你》

Among billions of worlds, I only found you.

初绽 (The First Bloom)

Like a rare flower in a boundless field, your smile brought color to my world. Even from so far away, your warmth always reaches me.

微风 (Gentle Breeze)

Every time I see your face, Jingxuan, it feels like a gentle spring breeze. You are the peace I never knew I was searching for.

繁花 (Blossoming)

They say distance makes the heart grow fonder, but my heart just grows more certain that you are the one I want to walk beside.

阳光小路 (Sunlit Path)

If I could plant a garden for every time I thought of you today, I would walk in my garden forever.

深海 (Deep Waters)

The oceans between us might seem vast, but my feelings for you run deeper than any tide.

思念的潮水 (Tides of Longing)

Like the ocean is pulled by the moon, I am endlessly drawn to you. Every wave that crashes feels like it's whispering your name.

清澈的倒影 (Clear Reflection)

When I look into your eyes, the whole world fades away. I see a beautiful future where there is no distance, only us.

坚如磐石 (Unshakable)

You are my strength. Just looking at your smile gives me the courage to climb any mountain and face any challenge.

迷人风景 (Breathtaking View)

From the highest peaks to the deepest valleys, nothing in this world compares to the beauty of your soul.

爱的回声 (Echoes of Love)

I want to shout my love for you so loud that the whole world hears it echoing back.

星空 (Constellations)

Out of all the stars in the sky, you shine the absolute brightest. You guide me through my darkest nights.

无尽星河 (Endless Galaxy)

The universe is infinite, but it still isn't big enough to hold how much I love you.

我最珍贵的宝物 (My Greatest Treasure)

Gold and diamonds mean nothing. Across all the servers, biomes, and worlds, finding you was the greatest discovery of my life.

《我愿意》 (I Do)

Hou Jingxuan, no matter the kilometers between us, no matter what obstacles we face, I promise to marry you.

I will cross every ocean and climb every mountain until the day I stand before you, slip a ring on your finger, and call you my wife. You are my forever.

Love, Mark.
